TAPIX

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 12, 2026

The short version: Tapix processes text you select in the keyboard extension by sending it to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered transformation. We do not collect personal information, we do not sell data, and we do not require an account. Text is sent only when you explicitly trigger an action, and is never stored after processing completes.

1. Who We Are

Tapix is developed and operated by Dmzlabz ("we," "us," or "our"). Tapix is an iOS keyboard extension that uses AI to transform your text — fix grammar, translate, adjust tone, shorten, and draft smart replies. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at the address at the bottom of this page.

2. What Data Tapix Processes

Tapix does not require an account. There is no registration, no login, and no user profile.

Text you select for AI actions. When you highlight text and tap an action (Fix It, Translate, Tone, Shorten, or Smart Reply), the selected text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. The text is used solely to perform the requested transformation and is not stored by Tapix after the result is returned.

Data stored on your device:

  • App preferences (selected language, notification settings, onboarding state)
  • AI action usage credits (stored via iCloud Key-Value Store and device Keychain to persist across reinstalls)
  • Subscription status (managed by Apple StoreKit)

None of this data is transmitted to Tapix's servers or any third party, except as described in Section 4.

3. Keyboard Extension Data Access

Tapix includes a custom keyboard extension. iOS requires "Full Access" to be enabled for the keyboard to communicate with the AI service. When Full Access is enabled:

  • Tapix can send the text you explicitly select to the Claude API when you trigger an action
  • Tapix does not log keystrokes, monitor typing, or record any text beyond what you actively select for an action
  • Tapix does not access your contacts, location, photos, or any other device data
  • No text is transmitted unless you explicitly tap an AI action button

The keyboard extension and the main app share data (preferences and credit balance) through Apple's App Groups framework. This communication stays entirely on your device.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

Tapix connects to the following external services. We use them only to provide specific features and we do not share personal information with any of them.

Anthropic Claude API — AI Text Transformation

When you trigger an AI action, the selected text is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. The text is used solely to perform the requested transformation (grammar correction, translation, tone adjustment, shortening, or smart reply generation). Tapix does not store the text after the result is returned. Anthropic may process this data subject to their own privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy

Apple StoreKit — In-App Subscriptions

Tapix Pro is available as a monthly or annual subscription through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. Tapix never sees your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions: apple.com/privacy

5. Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicit, Tapix does not collect or process:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Keystroke logs or typing patterns
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) for advertising purposes
  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendar entries, or health data
  • Behavioral analytics or usage telemetry
  • Crash logs sent to third-party services
  • Text you type but do not submit to an AI action

6. Bug Reports

If you choose to submit a bug report via the in-app "Report a Bug" feature, an email is opened on your device containing diagnostic information you can review before sending: app version, iOS version, device model, locale setting, and subscription status. This data is only sent if you choose to send the email. It is used solely to diagnose issues and is not shared with any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

Tapix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address it.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Tapix does not retain text submitted for AI processing. Text is sent to the API, the result is returned, and the original text is discarded.

App preferences and credit balances stored on your device are removed when you uninstall the app. Credit balances stored in iCloud Key-Value Store persist until you manually remove them from your iCloud storage via your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

9. Security

Tapix takes reasonable technical precautions to protect your data. All API communications use HTTPS. Sensitive preferences are stored in Apple's encrypted Keychain where applicable. The keyboard extension communicates with the main app exclusively through Apple's App Groups framework.

No method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to implementing best practices for an iOS application of this type.

10. International Users

Tapix is operated from Israel. If you are using the app from outside Israel, please be aware that text sent to the Anthropic Claude API may be processed in other jurisdictions, including the United States. By using Tapix, you consent to this processing.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Tapix after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will provide a notice within the app.

12. Your Rights

Because Tapix does not collect or store personal data on any server, most data-subject rights (access, portability, erasure) are fulfilled automatically by you controlling your own device. If you have questions about data processed by Anthropic (text transformations), please refer to their privacy policy and privacy portal.
