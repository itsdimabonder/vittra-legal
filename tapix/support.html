1. Getting Started

Setting up the Tapix Keyboard

  1. 1Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. 2Go to General > Keyboard > Keyboards > Add New Keyboard
  3. 3Select Tapix
  4. 4Tap Tapix again and enable Allow Full Access
  5. 5Full Access is required for the keyboard to communicate with AI providers over the network

Why does Tapix need Full Access?

Tapix uses AI providers (Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini) to transform your text. Full Access enables the keyboard to make network requests to these services. Without it, AI features cannot function.

Tapix does NOT log your keystrokes and does NOT collect passwords or sensitive information. The keyboard only reads text near your cursor when you explicitly trigger an AI action.

2. How to Use Tapix

From the Keyboard

  • Switch to the Tapix keyboard using the globe icon
  • Select or position your cursor near the text you want to transform
  • Choose an AI action (e.g., Rewrite, Fix Grammar, Translate, Summarize, Expand, etc.)
  • The transformed text will appear for you to review and use

From the App

  • Open Tapix and paste or type your text
  • Select an AI action
  • Review and copy the result

Available Actions

Tapix offers various AI-powered text transformations including rewriting, grammar correction, translation, summarization, expansion, tone adjustment, and more. Actions can be customized in the app settings.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

"Why aren't AI actions working?"

  • Ensure Full Access is enabled: Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards > Tapix > Allow Full Access
  • Check your internet connection — AI features require network access
  • If you've declined the AI consent prompt, go to Tapix app settings and accept it
  • Make sure you haven't reached your daily limit (15 actions for free users)

"I see 'Daily limit reached' — what do I do?"

  • Free users get 15 AI actions per day. The limit resets at midnight.
  • Upgrade to Tapix Premium for unlimited actions.

"The keyboard isn't showing up"

  • Make sure you've added the keyboard: Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards > Add New Keyboard > Tapix
  • Try restarting your iPhone
  • Ensure the app hasn't been offloaded (check Settings > General > iPhone Storage)

"AI results seem wrong or unexpected"

  • AI-generated text is produced by third-party models and may not always be perfect
  • Always review AI output before using it
  • Try rephrasing your input or using a different action for better results

"How do I change the translation language?"

Open Tapix > Settings > Translation Language and select your preferred target language.

"Can I use Tapix offline?"

No. Tapix requires an internet connection to communicate with AI providers. There is no offline mode.

6. Your Data & Privacy

What data does Tapix store?

  • A brief action history (80-character previews of input/output) stored locally on your device
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Anonymous usage analytics via Firebase

How to delete your data

  • Action History: Go to Tapix app > Action History and clear it
  • All Data: Uninstalling Tapix removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Analytics: You can limit analytics tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics

Does Tapix read everything I type?

No. The keyboard only reads text near your cursor when you explicitly trigger an AI action. Tapix does not log keystrokes or monitor your typing.

7. Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • Force close and reopen Tapix
  • Ensure you're running iOS 17 or later
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling

Keyboard is slow

  • Check your internet connection
  • AI processing may take a few seconds depending on text length and server load
  • Close unused apps to free up memory

"Network error" messages

  • Verify you have an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)
  • Ensure Full Access is enabled for the keyboard
  • Try again in a few moments — the AI service may be temporarily unavailable
