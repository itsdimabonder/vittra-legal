Getting Started

How do I enable the Tapix keyboard?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard → select Tapix. Then tap Tapix in the list and enable "Allow Full Access." Full Access is required for Tapix to send your selected text to the AI service for processing.

Is Full Access safe?

Yes. Tapix only sends text to the AI service when you explicitly tap an action button. We do not log keystrokes, monitor your typing, or access any text you don't actively select for an action. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Using Tapix

What AI actions are available?

Tapix offers five core actions: Fix It (grammar and spelling correction), Translate (between languages), Tone (with options for Formal, Casual, Polite, and Professional), Shorten (condense text while keeping meaning), and Smart Reply (generate a contextual response to a message).

How do I use an AI action?

Select text in any app, switch to the Tapix keyboard, and tap the action you want. The result appears in the keyboard — you can then accept it to replace your text or copy it to your clipboard.

Which languages does Tapix support?

Tapix currently supports Hebrew and English with full optimization. The AI model can process many other languages as well, though results may vary. We're actively expanding language support.

What are credits and how do they work?

Free users receive a limited number of daily AI action credits. Each time you trigger an action (Fix It, Translate, etc.), one credit is used. Credits reset daily. Pro subscribers get unlimited actions with no credit limits.

Tapix Pro

What does Tapix Pro include?

Tapix Pro unlocks unlimited AI actions (no daily credit limits), the Insights dashboard showing usage stats and time saved, priority processing, and all future Pro features. Available as a monthly or annual subscription.

How do I subscribe to Pro?

Open the Tapix app and tap the upgrade button. You can choose between monthly and annual plans. Payment is processed through Apple — we never see your payment details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Tapix, then tap "Cancel Subscription." You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Tapix, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Troubleshooting

The keyboard isn't showing up

Make sure you've added Tapix in Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards. If it's listed but not appearing, try removing and re-adding it. Restart your device if the issue persists.

Actions aren't working or returning errors

Check your internet connection — Tapix requires an active connection to process text. If connected but still failing, the AI service may be temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few minutes.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple's support at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I delete my data?

Tapix stores minimal data on your device (preferences and credit balance only). Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. Credit balances in iCloud Key-Value Store can be managed through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.
